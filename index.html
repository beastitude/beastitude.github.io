Design

My design philosophy is based in the idea that what I am designing is a character in the story of who I am designing for. As a character must have traits that shape their personality, my design must have characteristics that fit into the world a business has created for their brand. In doing so a design is efficient and feels right at home with users.

With my background in market research my design process is an example of such. Research from all perspectives is invaluable to generating concepts and ideas to move forward. I thrive on visual stimulation to learn and grow, so when generating ideas I am inspired by the designs of systems and their users in the real world such as public parks, the NYC subway system, and the architecture of man and nature. In past marketing campaigns, feedback is my secret to success.
